Easy Beef Strogonoff



  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cube beef bouillon
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 pint sour cream
  • 4 ounces egg noodles, cooked and drained


Directions


Steps:

  1. In a large skillet, brown beef in oil over medium-high heat. When meat is browned, drain excess fat from the skillet. Add garlic powder, salt, and pepper, and stir in.
  2. Add bouillon, onion, and mushrooms to the skillet and sauté until onions are translucent. Remove from heat (very important) and add sour cream. Stir all together and serve over hot cooked egg noodles.
