Easy Roast Beef Recipe



Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cube beef bouillon
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 pint sour cream
  • 4 ounces egg noodles, cooked and drained


Directions


Steps:

  • 3 pounds beef eye of round roast, at room temperature
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt, or more to taste
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder, or more to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, or more to taste

Directions

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C). Season the roast with salt, garlic powder, and pepper.
  2. Place the roast in a roasting pan and cook for 2-3 hours, or until a meat thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the roast reads 135°F (57°C).
  3. Remove the roast from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing.
